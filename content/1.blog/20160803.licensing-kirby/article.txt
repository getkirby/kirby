----

Date: 2016-08-03 00:00:00

Text: 

As you probably already know, Kirby is a commercial product. You can try it as long as you want on your local machine and the source code is available on (link: http://github.com/getkirby text: GitHub). But as soon as your want to use Kirby in production, you need to [purchase a license](https://getkirby.com/buy).

To keep Kirby affordable for as many people as possible, we have decided to make it available under a *personal* and a *commercial* license. The personal license may be used for personal / non-commercial projects only. The following chart may be helpful for you to decide which license is the right one for your project.
